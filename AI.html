AI in Gaming Animation
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the landscape of game animation in numerous ways, enhancing the realism, responsiveness, and overall experience of interactive media. Here's an in-depth look at the role of AI in game animation, covering its applications, technologies, and impacts on the industry.
Game animation involves creating the illusion of movement and behavior in characters and objects within a game. Traditionally, this was achieved through keyframe animation, where animators manually define the motion at certain points. However, AI has introduced more dynamic and responsive approaches to animation.
